About Us

NaoArtemis is a team of seven students from “Alle Stimate” High School in Verona, guided by Professor Giovanni Bellorio. United by a passion for technology and computer science, they aim to create real-world solutions that are both inclusive and sustainable. Curious and determined, they enjoy facing challenges and working as a team to turn bold ideas into reality. With their project, they’re developing new applications for NAO, a humanoid robot that makes sports more accessible for everyone.
