About Us

NaoArtemis is a team of seven students from “Alle Stimate” High School in Verona, guided by Professor Giovanni Bellorio. United by a passion for technology and computer science, they aim to create real-world solutions that are both inclusive and sustainable. Curious and determined, they enjoy facing challenges and working as a team to turn bold ideas into reality. With their project, they’re developing new applications for NAO, a humanoid robot that makes sports more accessible for everyone.

Project

Innovation in motion: robotics for athletes

NAO Coach & Care is the NaoArtemis project for the NAO Challenge 2025, designed to make sports more inclusive and personalized through robotics and AI. The first task focuses on supporting athletes with real-time feedback, motivation, and injury prevention. The second aims to assist fans with disabilities using Augmentative and Alternative Communication, making the sports experience accessible to everyone. In partnership with Audace, the team brings innovation and inclusion directly to the field.
